Howard AI support is built around real people, local technicians, and direct communication — not ticket queues or outsourced call centers.

Local Technicians

Every Howard installation and support visit is handled by a Houston-based technician. No third-party contractors. No shipping hardware across the country.

Real Communication

Call or text our support line and a real person answers. If your issue requires a visit, we dispatch someone directly to your home or office.

Because Howard runs locally on hardware inside your building, support is designed differently. Your data remains yours and never sits on a remote server.

Your subscription includes updates, hardware checks, technician dispatch, and long-term maintenance so Howard continues running smoothly over time.
