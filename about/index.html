About Howard AI

Assistant Intelligence.

Howard AI builds locally hosted AI assistants for Houston homes and businesses. Installed directly inside your location, Howard handles real tasks while keeping your data where it belongs — with you.

Built because cloud AI got it wrong.

Most AI assistants today live in distant cloud servers, collect enormous amounts of personal data, and still mostly give suggestions instead of actually doing things.



Howard was built around a different idea: your assistant should work for you, not a data center.

Locally Hosted

Howard runs entirely on dedicated hardware installed inside your home or office. Your conversations and data never leave your building.

Built to Execute

Howard doesn’t just answer questions. He books flights, manages schedules, handles communication, and completes real-world tasks.

Installed by People

Howard is professionally installed and maintained by local Houston technicians. No forums. No DIY frustration.

Privacy shouldn’t require trust.

Howard AI was designed around a simple principle: if customers care about privacy, they should physically control where their data lives.



Every Howard system is installed locally. There’s no cloud dependency storing conversations or personal information somewhere else. The privacy pitch is simple:



Don’t trust it?
Unplug it.

Built in Houston.

Howard AI is proudly Houston-based. Our support line is local. Our technicians are local. Our installations are local. We believe trust is built face-to-face, not through ticket numbers and outsourced call centers.



The long-term vision is simple: build the most trusted locally hosted AI service company in Texas, starting with Houston.
