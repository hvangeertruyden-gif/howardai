Executive Profile

Hendrik Van Geertruyden

Co-Founder

Hendrik Van Geertruyden is the Co-Founder of Howard AI and oversees the sales and business development aspect of the company.

Since co-founding Howard AI, Hendrik has been the architect of the company’s market presence and its unique "Assistant Intelligence" brand. He leads the teams responsible for growth and customer experience, ensuring that Howard AI’s values of privacy and reliability are reflected in every touchpoint. With his efforts, the company has pioneered a vertically integrated service model, inspired by Houston’s premier infrastructure providers, to deliver a premium, end-to-end AI experience that eliminates the friction of traditional cloud-based assistants.

Prior to his role at Howard AI, Hendrik held various leadership positions where he has a proven track record of project development and efficiency. His vision for Howard AI is rooted in the belief that true artificial intelligence should be a seamless, local service that empowers users through action and accountability. Hendrik holds a degree from no university at the current moment.
